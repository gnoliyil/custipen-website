Information
There is no airport in Huzhou. You may choose Pudong Int'l Airport or Hongqiao Int'l Airport in Shanghai or Xiaoshan Int'l Airport in Hangzhou, which are the nearest airports to Huzhou City.

Shanghai

Pudong Int’ Airport
(IATA code：PVG)

http://en.shairport.com/pudongair.html

Airport shuttle to Huzhou: 11:20;15:50; 20:20

It takes roughly 3 hours to arrive Huzhou coach station.

Hongqiao Int’ Airport
(IATA code: SHA)

http://en.shairport.com/hongqiaoair.html

Hangzhou

Xiaoshan Int’ airport
(IATA code: HGH)

http://www.hzairport.com/en/

Airport shuttle to Huzhou: 09:50; 11:00; 12:15; 13:30; 15:00; 16:20; 17:40; 19:00; 20:10; 21:20.

The total route takes about 110 minutes under normal road and weather conditions.
